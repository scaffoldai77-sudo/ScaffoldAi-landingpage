FAQ

Common questions

Is the desktop app really just 4MB?
Yes. We use Tauri instead of Electron. Tauri wraps your existing web UI inside the operating system's native browser engine — WebView2 on Windows and WKWebView on Mac. There is no bundled copy of Chromium, which is what makes Electron apps so large. The result is a 4–8MB installer that starts in under a second.
Does it work without an internet connection?
Partially. The desktop app caches your most-used templates locally, so basic scaffolding works offline. AI-powered generation (custom prompts, natural language input) requires an internet connection to process your prompt through our AI engine.
What stacks and languages are supported?
We support 40+ stacks across all major languages: JavaScript/TypeScript (React, Vue, Next.js, Node), Python (FastAPI, Django, Flask), Rust, Go, Flutter, React Native, Solidity, and many more. New templates are added every week based on community requests.
Can my team create custom templates?
Yes — this is a core feature of the Team plan. Your team defines your own project structures, coding conventions, and boilerplate rules. Every developer on the team scaffolds from your approved templates, enforcing consistency across every project automatically.
How is this different from Create React App or Django's startproject?
Framework CLIs are single-framework, single-purpose, and generate a fixed structure with no customization. ScaffoldAI is AI-powered, framework-agnostic, and understands your full project description — it picks the right template, customizes it to your exact needs, and can combine multiple frameworks into one full-stack project structure.
When does it launch? How much will it cost?
We're targeting a launch in Q3 2025. Pricing will start with a free tier (3 generations/month), Pro at $19/month (unlimited), and Team at $79/month. Everyone on the early access waitlist gets 3 months of Pro for free on launch day.
